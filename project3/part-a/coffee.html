Top 3 Coffee Recipes to Brew at Home

Explore our coffee brewing guide! Whether you're looking for homemade coffee recipes or want to brew barista-style coffee at home, you'll find everything you need to make rich and delicious coffee. Explore brewing methods and ingredients for different coffee types.

AMERICANO

Is an americano just black coffee? In short, no. An americano is a coffee drink that combines an espresso shot that is then diluted with water, as opposed to dripped coffee. The drink can be accompanied by either hot or cold milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • Hot water (approximate 150 ml)

Steps to Brew

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your coffee machine.
  2. Boil water and pour it into your cup.
  3. Add the espresso shot to the hot water.
  4. Enjoy your smooth and rich Americano!

LATTE

The name latte is derived from the Italian 'caffe latte', which translates to 'coffee milk'. It is an incredibly popular way to enjoy coffee, combining the intensity of a shot of espresso with silky steamed milk and a thin layer of foam.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 200ml of steamed milk
  • A small amount of milk foam (optional)

Steps to Brew

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your coffee machine.
  2. Steam the milk until it reaches a creamy texture making sure the temperature is around 65°C (149°F).
  3. Pour the steamed milk into your cup leaving a small space at the top for foam (if desired).
  4. Pour the espresso shot into the steamed milk.
  5. Optionally, top with a small amount of milk foam for a smooth finish.
  6. Enjoy your creamy and rich latte!

CAPPUCCINO

In Italy, the word cappuccino translates to 'little cap' or ‘hood’. The name originates from the Capuchin friars, where the colour of the Capuchin robe perfectly matches the mixture of the espresso and frothed milk in a cappuccino.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 100 ml of steamed milk
  • 100 ml of milk foam (frothy and dense)

Steps to Brew

  1. Brew a shot of espresso using your coffee machine.
  2. Steam the milk, but this time create more foam to give the cappuccino its signature frothy texture.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the cup, aiming to keep it dense.
  4. Add the espresso shot on top of the milk.
  5. Spoon a generous amount of milk foam on top of the espresso.
  6. Optionally, dust with cocoa powder or cinnamon for added flavor.
  7. Enjoy your foamy and strong cappuccino!
